My Story

Style has always felt like a quiet form of self-expression to me — intuitive, intentional, and powerful when it truly reflects who you are.

I’ve always believed that style is less about trends and more about intention. The way we dress can quietly shape how we move through the world, influencing confidence, presence, and ease. For me, fashion has always been a form of thoughtful self-expression.

Before working as a stylist, I spent years observing how people interact with their wardrobes — what they reach for, what they avoid, and why certain pieces make them feel more like themselves. That curiosity became the foundation of my approach: understanding the person first, then building the style around them. 

Today, I help clients simplify their wardrobes and refine their personal style through clarity and structure. My work blends intuition with strategy, creating looks that feel effortless, authentic, and aligned with real life — not just the moment.
