Our Story

How It All Began.

Founded in 2014, Groww Stylist was born from a simple realization: the fashion industry was heavily focused on models and mannequins, leaving everyday professionals overwhelmed by trends and cluttered closets.

What started as a small boutique consultancy in a studio apartment has now grown into a premier personal styling platform. We wanted to bridge the gap between high fashion and everyday life, showing people that authentic style is less about labels and more about self-expression and perfect fits.

Today, we've helped over 10,000 clients curate wardrobes that empower them to walk into any room feeling unstoppable.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize personal styling by making expert fashion advice accessible, personalized, and confidence-boosting for individuals of all body types, budgets, and lifestyles. We strive to educate our clients on what works best for them, fostering long-term style independence.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted personal styling platform, recognized for promoting sustainable wardrobe habits and transforming the way people perceive themselves through the powerful medium of clothing.
